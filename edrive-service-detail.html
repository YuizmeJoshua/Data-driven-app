What you'll learn

Once enrolled, you'll be paired with one of our experienced, certified instructors. They will be your personal guide throughout your driving journey. During your first session, your instructor will assess your current skill level and customize the lessons to fit your needs. Ready to get behind the wheel for the first time? Our Beginner Driver Course is designed for new drivers who are just starting their journey on the road. At Drive, we provide a comprehensive and supportive learning environment that equips you with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to become a safe and responsible driver.

Our beginner driver course covers all the essential skills and knowledge you need to pass your driving test and navigate the road with confidence:

Basic Vehicle Control: Learn how to start, stop, steer, and park with precision.

Traffic Laws & Road Signs: Understand the rules of the road, from speed limits to lane changes and everything in between.

Test Preparation: Focused practice to help you ace your driving test, with mock exams and tips to reduce test-day anxiety.

Highway & City Driving: Learn to drive confidently in both high-traffic and low-traffic conditions.

Parallel Parking: Master the art of parking, a crucial skill for urban driving and passing your road test.

Defensive Driving Techniques: Gain the skills to anticipate and react safely to potential hazards.

Course Features

Certified Instructors: All of our instructors are fully certified and have years of experience.

Flexible Scheduling: We understand that your time is valuable, so we offer flexible lesson times that fit into your schedule, including evenings and weekends.

Dual-Control Vehicles: For added safety, our cars are equipped with dual controls so that your instructor can intervene if necessary.
